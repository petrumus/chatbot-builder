Frequently Asked Questions

Most chatbots follow scripts — they present options, the user clicks, the bot presents more options. That's a decision tree with a chat interface. Our AI agent learns from your live website, generates contextually appropriate responses, and — in the full version — takes real actions in your real systems. It books appointments, updates CRMs, sends confirmations, and monitors open items. It's the difference between a phone menu and an employee who knows your business.

Five capability layers: (1) Task execution — books appointments, creates tickets, sends emails, updates records. (2) Proactive monitoring — watches for leads that haven't been followed up, tickets sitting unanswered, orders flagged for issues. (3) Autonomous decisions — enforces rules you define, like auto-escalating after missed responses. (4) Deep context — remembers customer history across sessions. (5) Open integrations — connects to anything online: CRMs, calendars, helpdesks, e-commerce, Slack, email.

The demo builds in under 60 seconds — just enter your website and a description. Full deployment with integrations and workflows takes a few days with our Setup Sprint ($499 one-time), where we map your workflows, configure everything, test, and deploy.
